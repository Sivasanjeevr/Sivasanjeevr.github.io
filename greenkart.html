GreenKart - Redefining E-commerce for Sustainable Shopping and Local Empowerment.

Greenkart redefines online shopping, offering a vast array of eco-friendly products with a focus on natural and organic goods. It's not your typical E-commerce platform. What sets Greenkart apart is its commitment to bridging the gap between producers and consumers, promoting sustainability, and nurturing a sense of community.

Tech Stack Used:

  • MERN stack - MongoDB , Express JS, React JS, Node JS

Greenkart employs the MERN stack , reinforced with secure user authentication through Auth0, and efficient cookie and token management for a seamless online shopping experience.
